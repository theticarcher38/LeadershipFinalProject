Extrinsic Motivation
vs.
Intrinsic Motivation



The whole premise of this game is to learn about extrinsic and intrinsic motivation
There are 5 levels to complete.
Each level has a scenario that outlines either intrinsic or extrinsic motivation
You must select the button of the type of motivation that the scenario is depicting.

You are at your workplace, and your boss incentivizes all employees to work harder by offering a bonus to all employees who show an increase in productivity. This is an example of which type of motivation?

Unfortunately that is not the correct answer. This would be an extrinsic form of motivation since the motivation is coming from an incentive offered by your boss.

You are studying for a test, and the only factor that is motivating you to keep studying is a fear of failure. Is this a form of extrinsic or intrinsic motivation?

While it is technically you motivating yourself to keep studying, this would still be a form of extrinsic motivation as it is a fear of failure that is motivating you to study.

You are working on a project, and your motivation to keep you going is the fact that you enjoy the topic that the project is covering. What type of motivation is this?

This would be intrinsic motivation as the motivation for the project comes from an inner desire to do the project.

You are helping someone out for the sole purpose of receiving praise from family and friends. What type of motivation is this?

This is extrinsic motivation since you are helping someone out in order to receive praise which is an external reward.

You feel motivated to participate is a certain sport because you find that sport to be fun. What type of motivation is this?

This would be intrinsic motivation because you are participating in the sport because you think that it is fun.
